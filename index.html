In 2014, Ethan Marcotte suggested the need for a framework for responsive web design, one that was not about execution, but about philosophy and quality. This framework would help us to frame the continuing discussion about our practice, and also act as a reference point for measuring the appropriateness of our work.

We can start the work of building this framework, by agreeung upon a set design principles that underly our practice, each of which works in service of a broader goal, that of building a web that remains accessible to all.
